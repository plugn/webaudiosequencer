Web Audio Sequencer

Make music with sounds from around the web using HTML5 and the Web Audio API.

To audition a sound, click the play button to the left of its title.

Once you've found a sound you like, click the + button to the right of its title.

You will be prompted to choose one of the following options:

  • Melodic - the layer will feature one sound playable at varying pitches (like a piano).
  • Percussive - the layer will feature one sound playable at one pitch, with room to stack additional sounds (like a drumkit).

Then the sequence interface will appear and you can begin making music. Have fun!
